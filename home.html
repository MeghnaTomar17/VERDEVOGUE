About Us

VerdeVogue is a brand that embodies the spirit of sustainable fashion. With a focus on environmentally friendly materials and ethical production practices, VerdeVogue aims to revolutionize the fashion industry by offering stylish, eco-conscious clothing. Our collections are designed with nature in mind, using recycled fabrics, organic materials, and upcycled garments to reduce waste and promote a greener future. At VerdeVogue, we believe that fashion should not only reflect personal style but also contribute to a positive impact on the planet. Join us on our mission to make the world a better place, one outfit at a time.

About Our App

VerdeVogue’s mobile app brings sustainable fashion right to your fingertips. With a focus on eco-conscious shopping, our app allows you to browse our full collection, make purchases, track your environmental impact, and stay connected with the latest in sustainable fashion trends. Whether you’re looking for upcycled garments, vegan leather options, or eco-friendly fabrics, VerdeVogue makes it easy to shop responsibly.

The app also offers a personalized shopping experience, tailoring product recommendations based on your preferences and past purchases. Additionally, it features a reward system for eco-friendly actions, like recycling old clothes and opting for sustainable materials. With VerdeVogue, you can shop with a clear conscience, knowing that your choices are contributing to a more sustainable fashion industry.

Our Story – The Birth of VerdeVogue

VerdeVogue began as a passion project sparked by a simple yet powerful question: “Can fashion look good, feel good, and do good for the planet?” Founded by a group of college students driven by climate awareness and creativity, VerdeVogue was born out of a desire to challenge the fast fashion cycle and give people a better alternative. We saw how quickly trends changed—and how much waste was left behind. What started as a sustainability-themed hackathon idea soon evolved into a mission: To make sustainable fashion not just a choice, but a lifestyle. We merged tech and consciousness—developing smart tools like TrashScan, WasteWatch, and BinSense—to help individuals make informed, impactful fashion choices every day. With VerdeVogue, our goal is to educate, empower, and inspire a new generation of shoppers to walk a little lighter on the earth, while staying stylish. This is more than a brand. It’s a movement. It’s a mindset. It’s VerdeVogue.
